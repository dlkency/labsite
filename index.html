Innovating hazard management for a resilient future.

At the forefront of research, PI Dan Li leads a dedicated team at Florida International University. Our lab specializes in investigating the cascade effects of natural hazards across interconnected water resources, infrastructure, and economic frameworks.

By employing cutting-edge AI-enabled modeling techniques, we translate complex risk assessments into practical strategies for mitigation and adaptation. Our mission is to prepare communities for foreseeable challenges while fostering sustainable development in the face of adversity.

We are excited to announce recruitment at all student levels, inviting passionate minds to join our impactful research endeavors.
